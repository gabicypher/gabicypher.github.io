    Sketchbook

    This page is just a bunch of my drawings from the past decade or so of people and places that are important to me--but none of my intentional or professional art. Everything here has been a little bit crumpled or accidentally thrown away, and everything was made with so much love ❤️

    These drawings are mostly pen doodles from my sketchbook, but some are on printer paper or whatever scraps I could find lying around with whatever materials I could scrounge (highlighter, Crayola markers, stolen pencils, etc). The digital drawings are in MS Paint or sometimes FireAlpaca.
